Writing

I also love writing - the way I "talk to myself" and store my thoughts, experiences, and what I learned from open courses. As any graduate student, I usually face academic problems (stress, loss of motivation, and not knowing how to work/study efficiently). I have been collecting knowledge and experience from senior people to cope with these issues. In the beginning, I just wanted to write things down for myself. However, I felt that may be helpful for other students who are facing the same obstacles as I did. Therefore, I would like to share what I've collected, especially about how to self-motivate under academic pressure, in Life of a grad student. (In that blog, you can also find some useful tools for your research in this page .)
